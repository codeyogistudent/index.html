About Us

Kamlapur Govt. Higher Secondary School was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education. Our goal is to create an environment where students can learn, grow, and excel academically as well as personally.

We focus on holistic development, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and leadership skills among students. Our commitment to inclusivity and innovation has made us a center of learning excellence.
